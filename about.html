Our Founding Story

HikmaKasozi General Hardware was founded in 2023 with a vision to bridge the gap between affordability and quality in building materials. Starting from a small shop in town, our commitment to reliability, customer service, and durable products has helped us grow into a trusted supplier for builders and homeowners across the region.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, affordable building solutions that meet the needs of both large-scale contractors and everyday builders while promoting integrity and excellence in service.

Our Vision

To be East Africa’s most trusted and innovative hardware provider—empowering communities to build better homes, businesses, and futures.
